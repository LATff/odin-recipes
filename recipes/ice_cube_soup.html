Icecube Soup

Perfect for those balmy summer days.

This recipe makes as many portions as you can stand.

A dish the whole family can enjoy, from grandma to the dog. Ice cube soup throws wide the gates regarding the esoteric debate of what a soup actually is.

Why not invite your loved ones over and encourage them to partake in what is sure to be a delightful discussion on the matter.

Ingredients

  • 1 handful icecubes per person
  • 1 bowl water per person
  • Sprig of mint, or cherry tomato to garnish

    • Preparation

    1. Fill bowl with water.
    2. Add icecubes.
    3. Serve IMMEDIATELY.
